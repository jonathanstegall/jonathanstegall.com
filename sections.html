Work

My life is rooted in theology, faith community, and radical organizing. I try to fit my work into these areas as I can, and to provide others working for justice with useful access to design.

Day job

Since late 2014, I've led user experience design and web development at MinnPost, a news nonprofit in Minneapolis. Read more about some of my work experience.

Consulting

I consult with organizers, non-profits, and faith communities around how they design experiences, digital or otherwise.

Most recently I've done user-centered strategic work with the Center for Sustainable Justice.

Workshop

I offer a full-day workshop that introduces design, and the practices of design strategy, design research, and prototyping to non-designers. I love sharing these skills with activists, organizers, and faith leaders.

Writing
