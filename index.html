Hi, I'm Jonathan Stegall. I'm a designer, developer, and organizer in Minneapolis.

I work as a designer and developer. I have skills and experience in design research and strategy, information architecture, interface design, and web development.

I've been a user experience leader, designer, developer, ecommerce specialist, technologist, and team mentor.

I build, lead, and grow user-centered design practices within organizations.

Work

I lead user experience design and web development at MinnPost, a news nonprofit in Minneapolis. Much of our web work is open source. I also consult with and teach organizers, non-profits, and faith communities about design, digital and otherwise. Read about my work.

Writing

Since 2007 I've maintained an occasional blog. I write about theology, design, activism, and their overlaps. I also tweet a lot, and sometimes write on Facebook. Read my writing.

Reading

I like to share about what I read, whether online or in print. Read about what I've been reading.

About

My life is rooted in theology, faith community, and radical organizing. This life is always informing and informed by my work as a designer, and I integrate them as I can. I design this website to reflect that integration. Read about me and my website.
